# PROBOOTH 📸

PROBOOTH is a simple web-based photo booth app built using **HTML, CSS, and JavaScript**.  
It allows users to capture photos using their camera, apply filters, choose layouts, add stickers, and download the final image.

## Features
- Camera preview
- Photo timer (3s, 5s, 10s)
- Multiple layouts:
  - Solo photo
  - 3-photo strip
  - 2x2 grid
- Filters (Black & White, Vintage, Cool, Warm)
- Mirror camera mode
- Custom caption
- Custom frame color
- Drag and resize emoji stickers
- Download final image as PNG

## How to Use
1. Open `index.html` in your browser.
2. Click **Camera** to enable your webcam.
3. Choose layout, timer, and filter.
4. Click **Start Session** to capture photos.
5. Add stickers if you want.
6. Click **Save Final Image** to download.
